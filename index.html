How to Play

Crack the Master using three Sub‑Clues. Each wrong guess costs a life.
  • Start with 3 sub‑clues. Each wrong sub‑guess reveals one new hint (max 5).
  • You can attempt the Master at any time. Wrong = lose a life.
  • Solving a sub‑clue reveals all its hints.
  • Win by solving the Master before you run out of lives.
